// the mythos

GODDOITA

Japanese for "God Eater"  ·  releasing August 2026

It started with a fever dream — chasing a purple cat through a world that wouldn't hold still. That cat became Cheshire: a paradox that was never supposed to exist, a walking contradiction to every law the universe tries to enforce.

Goddoita is his story — him and an unlikely crew trying to heal him from his own unraveling, before the universe corrects itself the only way it knows how: by erasing what shouldn't exist.
